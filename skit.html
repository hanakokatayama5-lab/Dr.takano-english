英会話スキット学習

実践的なやり取りと入れ替え練習

Clean Example

1. 基本の会話例

Staff: What seems to be the problem today?
Patient: My lower back hurts.
Staff: I see. Try not to sit for too long. Please move around a little during the day.
Patient: Okay.
Staff: Please do some light stretching at home. If the pain gets worse, please come back.
Patient: Thank you.

Substitution Practice

3. 入れ替え練習用テンプレート

Staff: What seems to be the problem today?
Patient: My 【1. 痛い場所】 hurts.
Staff: I see. Try not to 【2. 気をつけること】. Please move around a little during the day.
Patient: Okay.
Staff: Please 【3. 家での対応】 at home. If the pain gets worse, please come back.
Patient: Thank you.

1. 痛い場所

  • lower back (腰)
  • shoulder (肩)
  • neck (首)

2. 気をつけること

  • sit for too long (長時間座りすぎる)
  • lift heavy things (重いものを持つ)
  • use your phone for too long (スマホ長時間使用)

3. 家での対応

  • do some light stretching (ストレッチ)
  • rest (安静)
  • ice the area (冷やす)
